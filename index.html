Digital Skills Training Program for Healthcare Professionals Practical Applications of Generative Video Production

Hsiao-Chi Wu, I-Ling Yang, Liu Wen, Shu-Yi Hsu, Yen-Ju Wang, Liu Wen, Ya-Wen Tsai
Chi Mei Medical Center, Tainan, Taiwan

Background

Generative video tools are increasingly being applied in healthcare workflows, including:

  • Patient education
  • Workflow optimization
  • Professional communication

Challenge: Many healthcare professionals lack familiarity, confidence, and positive attitudes toward these tools, limiting their effective application.

Study Aim: To evaluate the impact of a targeted educational program on participants' knowledge, attitudes, and skills.
